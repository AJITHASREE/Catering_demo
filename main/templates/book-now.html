Questions Answered

Booking FAQs

How soon should I book in advance?
For weddings, we recommend 2-3 months in advance. For other events, 2-4 weeks is typically sufficient. However, we always try to accommodate last-minute requests based on availability.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash, bank transfer, cheque, UPI, and online payment methods. A deposit is required to confirm the booking, with the balance due before the event.
Can I change the booking details later?
Yes, you can modify guest count, menu, or other details up to 2 weeks before the event. Changes made closer to the event may incur additional charges.
What's the cancellation policy?
Cancellations made 30 days in advance receive a full refund. Cancellations within 30 days of the event may incur a cancellation charge based on our policy.
Do you offer tasting sessions before booking?
Yes! For weddings and large events, we offer complimentary tasting sessions after the initial consultation. This helps you finalize your menu with confidence.
