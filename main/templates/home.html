FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum number of guests for catering?
We can cater events starting from 15-25 guests depending on the service type. For weddings, we prefer a minimum of 50 guests. Please contact us for customized quotes.
Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?
Absolutely! We accommodate vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and other dietary requirements. Please inform us during the consultation so we can plan accordingly.
How far in advance should we book?
For weddings, we recommend booking 2-3 months in advance. For other events, 2-4 weeks is typically sufficient. However, we always try to accommodate last-minute requests based on availability.
Do you provide servers and waitstaff?
Yes, professional service staff is included in our wedding and corporate packages. For smaller events, it's available as an add-on.
